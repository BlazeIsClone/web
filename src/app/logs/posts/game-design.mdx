### Love for Video Games

I love games, I really did for the longest time I could remember, all my childhood I would rush back home from shchool to hop into my computer to play games. From 2D platforms, racing, action or simulation I happen to enjoy them all. It was never an activity that I did to pass my time but it took me away into a different world each told a different story that gave me a purpose and a super power to assist me through it all.

- Interactive chocies
- Creative story telling
- Immersive environments
- Competitive and cooperative play

Most games focuses on delivering few of the above aspects while trying to capture the players interest.

The player always drives the game forward.

### What Goes Into A Video Game

Creating a video game is a highly multidisciplinary process that blends technical engineering and creative arts.

- Art
- Design
- Engineering
- Audio
- Writing
- Testing
